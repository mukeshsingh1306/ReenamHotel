Staying in Ladakh with Reenam Hotel

If you are looking for a stay in Leh that balances budget and comfort, Reenam Hotel is designed to give you good value without cutting back on the essentials. Rooms are clean, centrally heated and thoughtfully planned so you can rest well between drives and sightseeing.

Most rooms come with the practical touches you need in the mountains – warm bedding, attached bathrooms, reliable hot water and enough storage space for winter layers and travel gear. The idea is to keep things simple, homely and comfortable rather than overly formal.

The team can also help connect you to trusted local taxis, simple restaurants and day trips so you can plan your Leh–Ladakh days without having to research everything from scratch.

In the in-house dining area you will find straightforward meals made with local produce – from Ladakhi dishes to familiar Indian and continental options – so you can eat well and head out again without worrying about where to find your next meal.

Questions guests often ask

  • Why do budget travellers choose Reenam Hotel?

    Many guests find that the hotel offers a practical balance of price and comfort – clean, heated rooms, basic modern amenities and helpful staff, without the feel of a large, impersonal property.

  • What kind of facilities do the rooms have?

    Rooms are set up with central heating, comfortable beds, attached bathrooms with hot and cold water, fresh linen and simple furniture so you can unpack properly and feel at home during your stay.

  • Can the hotel help with local travel and sightseeing?

    Yes. The team can point you to reliable taxis and operators for airport transfers, local sightseeing and popular circuits such as Nubra, Pangong and Sham Valley.

  • Is the location convenient for exploring Leh town?

    The hotel is in Chubi, about one and a half kilometres uphill from the main Leh market, with easy access by car or a short walk. Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace are also within comfortable driving distance.

  • Does the hotel serve local Ladakhi food?

    The kitchen prepares freshly cooked meals, including Ladakhi dishes and simple global favourites, using seasonal ingredients so you can try local flavours while still finding something familiar if you prefer.
