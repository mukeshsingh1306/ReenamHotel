Common room amenities

Across categories, rooms share a core set of practical comforts designed for Ladakh’s climate and long travel days.

  • King or queen‑size beds with warm bedding and electric blankets
  • Towels, basic toiletries and attached bathrooms with 24‑hour hot and cold water
  • Tea/coffee maker, intercom and flat‑screen TV with DTH connection
  • Work desk, chairs, a pair of easy chairs or sofa seats and a cupboard
  • Centrally heated building with bed warmers for colder nights
  • In‑house laundry service, daily housekeeping and a luggage room
  • Free internet access and room service within designated hours
