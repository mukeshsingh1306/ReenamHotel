Facilities

Practical comforts like central heating, hot water and Wi‑Fi, combined with quiet spaces to relax.

  • Warm, comfortable rooms

    Centrally heated rooms with king or twin beds, fresh linen, electric blankets and attached bathrooms with 24‑hour hot and cold water.

  • Connectivity & services

    Free Wi‑Fi in common areas, intercom in rooms, daily housekeeping, luggage room and in‑house laundry service.

  • Spaces to unwind

    Garden sit‑outs, lobby seating and mountain views that let you slow down between sightseeing, with staff on hand to help with taxis and local tips.
