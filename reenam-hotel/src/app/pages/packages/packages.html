Leh Ladakh tour packages

Reenam Hotel curates multi-day Leh–Ladakh journeys that combine stays at the hotel with transport, sightseeing and time to explore the region at a comfortable pace.

You can choose from the sample packages below or share your travel dates and broad plan with the team so they can design a tailor-made itinerary that fits your interests, season and number of days in Ladakh.

Ladakh – Lake & Silk Route

05 nights / 06 days Leh · Nubra Valley · Pangong Lake

A compact route that gives you time in Leh, a night in Nubra and a full-day excursion to Pangong Lake, driving over high passes and visiting key monasteries along the way.

View sample 6-day flow
  • Day 1: Arrive in Leh, transfer to the hotel, acclimatise and take an easy evening stroll to the market and Shanti Stupa.
  • Day 2: Indus valley monastery circuit with stops such as Shey, Thiksey, Stok Palace and Sindhu Ghat.
  • Day 3: Drive across Khardung La into Nubra Valley, with time for Diskit Monastery and the dunes near Hunder.
  • Day 4: Return to Leh over Khardung La and keep the afternoon free to rest or browse the local market.
  • Day 5: Full-day drive to Pangong Lake via Changla pass with time along the shore before heading back to Leh.
  • Day 6: Transfer to Leh airport for your onward flight.

Amazing Ladakh

06 nights / 07 days Leh · Nubra Valley · Pangong Lake · Alchi

A slightly longer circuit that layers in visits to Alchi, Hall of Fame, Magnetic Hill and the Indus–Zanskar confluence, along with Nubra and Pangong.

View sample 7-day flow
  • Day 1: Arrive in Leh, settle in at the hotel and stroll to the market and Shanti Stupa in the evening.
  • Day 2: Full-day excursion on the Srinagar highway covering Hall of Fame, Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Magnetic Hill, the Indus–Zanskar confluence and Alchi.
  • Day 3: Drive over Khardung La into Nubra Valley with sightseeing around Samstaling or nearby monasteries.
  • Day 4: Visit Diskit and the sand dunes before returning to Leh over Khardung La.
  • Day 5: Drive to Pangong Lake via Changla and stay near the lake.
  • Day 6: Return to Leh, stopping at Thiksey and Shey on the way back.
  • Day 7: Departure from Leh.

Lakes of Ladakh

07 nights / 08 days Leh · Nubra Valley · Pangong Lake · Tsomoriri

An itinerary focused on Ladakh’s high–altitude lakes, adding Tsomoriri to the classic Leh–Nubra– Pangong combination.

View sample 8-day flow
  • Day 1: Arrive in Leh, acclimatise and explore the market and Shanti Stupa.
  • Day 2: Cross Khardung La into Nubra Valley and check into a camp or resort.
  • Day 3: Visit dunes and monasteries in Nubra before returning to Leh.
  • Day 4: Drive to Pangong Lake via Changla and stay close to the lake.
  • Day 5: Morning at Pangong, then return to Leh with monastery stops en route.
  • Day 6: Leh to Tsomoriri via Chumathang, with time at the hot springs and lakeside village.
  • Day 7: Scenic drive back from Tsomoriri to Leh.
  • Day 8: Departure from Leh.

Lakes of Ladakh with Uleytokpo

09 nights / 10 days Leh · Uleytokpo · Pangong Lake · Nubra Valley · Tsomoriri

A more expansive circuit that adds Uleytokpo and Lamayuru to the lakes and valleys, with extra nights to travel at an easier pace.

View sample 10-day flow
  • Day 1: Arrive in Leh, rest and evening market visit.
  • Day 2: Leh to Uleytokpo with stops at Phyang, Likir, Basgo and Alchi.
  • Day 3: Excursion to Lamayuru and return to Leh.
  • Day 4: Drive from Leh to Pangong Lake via Changla and stay by the lake.
  • Day 5: Morning by the lake, then return to Leh with monastery visits en route.
  • Day 6: Leh to Nubra Valley over Khardung La and evening at the camp.
  • Day 7: Explore dunes and monasteries in Nubra before returning to Leh.
  • Day 8: Leh to Tsomoriri via Chumathang hot springs.
  • Day 9: Return from Tsomoriri to Leh for a final night in town.
  • Day 10: Departure from Leh.

These sample Leh Ladakh tour packages are designed to give you a feel of the region’s monasteries, markets, high passes, lakes and valleys without rushing through the sights.

Whether you prefer a shorter city-and-valley break or a longer circuit that connects multiple lakes, the team can help fine-tune the number of nights at each stop and suggest the right season to visit.
